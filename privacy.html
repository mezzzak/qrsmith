QRSMITH is designed as a client-side utility. This means that the text or URLs you enter to generate QR codes are processed entirely within your web browser. We do not send, store, or see the data you use to create your QR codes on our servers.

Note that QRSMITH has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
